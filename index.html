About Us

Acol Chemical Holdings has two major divisions namely Acol Chemical and Technical Services Africa (TSA). Acol Chemical specializes in industrial raw materiats and mining chemicals.

  • Acol Chemical is the major supplier of raw materials in Zimbabwe and acts as both a distributor and agent with its offices located in Harare and Bulawayo
  • With a team of qualified sates personnel, Acol boasts the largest chemical sates force in the country, enabling it to provide a first class service on behatf of its Principals and to provide technical back up to its customers
  • Acol is particularly strong in the plastics, packaging, mining, cosmetics, detergents, potyurethanes, GRP, paint, textile, paper, leather and rubber business. lt is atso one of the major suppliers of crop protection chemicals to the agriculturaI sector.

Technical Services (Africa) is one of the leading suppliers of agricultural chemicals in Zimbabwe and the region.

  • Companies represented include, Karbochem, NCS Resins, Sasol, Clariant, Betachem, Associated Additives, Evonik Peroxide, BASF Polyurethanes, Bayer Crop Science, Makhteshim Agan, Safripol, Ferro and major manufacturers in the Far East.
  • Through its common shareholdings with Ominia/Protea group, Acol also has access to the huge range of chemicals handled by them in South Africa and sourced from overseas
  • Acol has invested heavily in warehousing, bulk storage, transportation, laboratories and skilled human resources. Combined with its excellent portfolio of world leading multinational and South Africa chemical Principals, Acol is the chemical supplier of choice in Zimbabwe.
